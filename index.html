Kawasaki Z900 is a 948cc sports naked bike with price starting from Rs. 9.38 lakh. It is available in 2 colours and is offered in a single variant. Z900 mileage is 17 kmpl. The bike gets double disc brakes in the front and disc brakes at the rear, apart from this it weighs 212 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 17 liters. Z900 is a tough competitor to Ducati Monster .
